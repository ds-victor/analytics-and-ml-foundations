# Business Analytics Cheat Sheet

A quick reference guide for essential Business Analytics concepts, Excel shortcuts, and KPI formulas.

## 1. BA Frameworks
- **SMART Goals:** Specific, Measurable, Actionable, Relevant, Time-bound.
- **RACI Matrix:** Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed.
- **DDDM Cycle:** Define → Identify Data → Analyze → Decide → Measure → Iterate.

## 2. Essential Excel Shortcuts
- **PivotTable:** `Alt + N + V`
- **Absolute Reference ($):** `F4`
- **Filter:** `Ctrl + Shift + L`
- **AutoSum:** `Alt + =`
- **VLOOKUP Syntax:** `=VLOOKUP(lookup_value, table_array, col_index, [range_lookup])`
- **XLOOKUP Syntax:** `=XLOOKUP(lookup_value, lookup_array, return_array)`

## 3. Common KPI Formulas
- **ROI (Return on Investment):** `(Net Profit / Investment Cost) * 100`
- **Profit Margin:** `(Net Income / Revenue) * 100`
- **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** `Total Marketing Spend / New Customers Acquired`
- **Churn Rate:** `(Customers Lost / Total Customers at Start) * 100`
- **LTV (Lifetime Value):** `Average Order Value * Purchase Frequency * Customer Lifespan`

## 4. Visual Selection Guide
- **Trend over time:** Line Chart
- **Comparison of categories:** Bar Chart
- **Relationship between values:** Scatter Plot
- **Part-to-whole:** Stacked Bar (Avoid Pie Charts for >2 categories)
- **KPI Performance:** Gauge or Bullet Chart

## 5. Stakeholder Communication
- **Executive Summary:** Keep it high-level, focus on ROI and recommendations.
- **Technical Teams:** Provide detailed requirements and logic.
- **End Users:** Focus on how the tool/dashboard helps their daily workflow.
