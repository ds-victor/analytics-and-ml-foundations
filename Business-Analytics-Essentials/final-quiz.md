# Final Quiz: Business Analytics Essentials

Test your understanding of the business analyst role, KPIs, and data-driven decision-making.

## Questions

1. **Which of the following best describes the primary goal of a Business Analyst?**
   - A) Writing complex Python scripts for deep learning.
   - B) Bridging the gap between business needs and data solutions.
   - C) Managing the company's database infrastructure.
   - D) Designing user interfaces for mobile apps.

2. **What does the 'M' in SMART objectives stand for?**
   - A) Manageable
   - B) Motivational
   - C) Measurable
   - D) Multitasking

3. **Which KPI category would 'Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)' fall under?**
   - A) Financial
   - B) Operational
   - C) Customer
   - D) Sales

4. **A 'Lagging Indicator' is a metric that:**
   - A) Predicts future performance.
   - B) Measures performance after the event has occurred.
   - C) Tracks real-time data only.
   - D) Is always better than a leading indicator.

5. **In Excel, which tool is best for summarizing and aggregating large datasets?**
   - A) VLOOKUP
   - B) Conditional Formatting
   - C) PivotTables
   - D) Data Validation

6. **Which type of analytics answers the question "What should we do?"**
   - A) Descriptive
   - B) Diagnostic
   - C) Predictive
   - D) Prescriptive

7. **A 'Stakeholder' in a BA project is:**
   - A) Only the CEO.
   - B) Anyone affected by or having an interest in the project outcome.
   - C) The data engineering team only.
   - D) An external consultant.

8. **When presenting to executives, your visuals should primarily focus on:**
   - A) Complex technical details of the SQL queries.
   - B) High-level insights and clear recommendations.
   - C) 3D effects and bright colors.
   - D) Raw data tables.

9. **Which tool would you choose if your company primarily uses the Microsoft Office 365 ecosystem?**
   - A) Tableau
   - B) Looker
   - C) Power BI
   - D) Qlik

10. **Data-driven decision making (DDDM) helps to:**
    - A) Eliminate all risks.
    - B) Reduce bias and improve objective outcomes.
    - C) Replace human intuition entirely.
    - D) Make decisions slower but more expensive.
